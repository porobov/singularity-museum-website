Million Ether Homepage

An advertising platform on the surface. Deeper meaning undermined. A project exploring the nature of humanity. TheMillionEtherHomePage.com (MEH) is an advertising platform launched at the dawn of web3 era (launched 13 December 2016). It allows to buy an area at 1000x1000 pixels field and place ads within that area. An ad is an image with a link to a website. In other words the project is a decentralised and trustless version of milliondollarhomepage.com (from the dawn of web2 era).

Upala

Upala is a decentralized digital identity. The protocol generates human uniqueness score for users. The score is measured in dollars. Each account score is determined by market. Incentives ensure that the score equals the costs of forging the account. The score directly reflects how valuable that identity is to the user and creates an entirely new way to measure reputability in the virtual and real world.
